About Mr. Robot

Mr. Robot is a gripping drama that revolves around Elliot Anderson, a super-hacker nerd with clinical depression and Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). His alter-ego, named Mr. Robot, is cast in his father’s image due to Elliot's daddy issues. Throughout all four seasons, Elliot seeks to recast the unjust social order, where the top 1% of 1% enjoy all the privileges of the world at the cost of others.
