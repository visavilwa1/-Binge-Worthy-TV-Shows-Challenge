Mr. Robot

Elliot Anderson, a super-hacker nerd, with clinical depression and Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). His alter-ego is named Mr Robot, who is casted in his father’s image, since Elliot has daddy-issues. Through all four seasons, he seeks to recast the unjust social order where top 1% of 1% enjoy all the privileges of the world at the cost of others.
